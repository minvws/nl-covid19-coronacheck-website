<details class="details">
<summary><h2>In the CoronaCheck app</h2></summary>
<div markdown="1">
New vaccinations are not retrieved automatically in the app. Therefore, you have to add every jab yourself. In the menu, choose “Add vaccination or test” and retrieve your vaccination details by logging in with DigiD.
</div>
</details>

<details class="details">
<summary><h2>To your paper certificate </h2></summary>
<div markdown="1">
Go to [www.coronacheck.nl/en/print](/en/print) and create a new vaccination certificate. Your new jab will be added to your paper certificate.
</div>
</details>
